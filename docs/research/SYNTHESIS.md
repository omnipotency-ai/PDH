# Research Synthesis -- PDH

This document indexes all research files in `docs/research/` against the product vision defined in `docs/VISION.md`. PDH is a digestive recovery tracker for post-reconnective surgery patients. The vision centres on seven pillars: Logging, Bristol Stool Scale, Food-Digestion Correlation, AI Insights, Authentication and Privacy, Health Profile and Settings, and Testing. Each research file is summarised, tagged with its implementation status, and mapped to the pillar(s) it supports.

---
