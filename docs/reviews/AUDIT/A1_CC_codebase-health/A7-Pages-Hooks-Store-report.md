## Store Shape Assessment

**Overall:** Clean and well-structured.

**Selector granularity:** Good. All consumers select specific state slices — no consumer selects the entire store object.

**Action co-location:** Good. All actions are defined inline in the `create()` call.

**One concern:** Type guards (`isFoodLog`, `isFluidLog`, etc.) live in `store.ts` but have nothing to do with the Zustand store — they are utilities for narrowing `LogEntry` unions used widely across the codebase. This is a misplaced export.
